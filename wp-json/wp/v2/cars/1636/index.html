No list of luxury cars is complete without an entrant from Rolls-Royce, is it? The legendary luxury automaker would have been left off this list of the most luxurious SUVs a few years ago, when they only made coupes and sedans, but after driving the Rolls-Royce Cullinan, it’s obvious the newest Rolls deserves a spot.<\/p>\n

\n

It’s got the coach doors. There’s a Rolls-Royce umbrella tucked inside the door. Not only are there champagne flutes and a bottle refrigerator, there are two Rolls-Royce highball glasses and a decanter for the elusive, barrel-aged, single malt whiskey gifted to you by a member of the royal family. As far as luxurious SUVs go, the Cullinan is a
